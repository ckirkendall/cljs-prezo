Macros

Macros are the difference between having a tool box and owning a machine shop.

Enfocus:

(defaction setup-pane [width height]
     ["#menu"] (delay 1000 (do->
                             (set-style :display "inline")
                             (fade-in 1000)))
     ["#content-pane"] (chain
                         (resize 5 height 500)
                         (resize width :curheight 500)
                         (content (home))))
